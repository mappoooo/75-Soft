How 75 SOFT works

A handful of daily habits, seventy-five days, one street full of people quietly cheering you on.

Your daily list

  • 10,000 steps — logged manually (see note below).
  • 3 litres of water — yes, all of it.
  • 10 pages of a self-help / growth book.
  • No alcohol or soft drink — sparkling water is not a loophole, but it's also not against the rules.
  • No added sugar or takeaway — home-cooked only, and check labels for the sneaky stuff. Natural sugar in whole fruit is fine. Check the Foods tab if you're unsure.
  • One 45-minute workout, every day, no swaps.
  • Optional second 45-minute session — toggle this on yourself. It's your call, and only your call.

Why steps are manual

A web app like this one can't be granted access to Apple Health or an Apple Watch — that data is locked to Apple's own Health app and apps you've specifically approved on your phone. Log your count from your Watch or Health app each day; it takes five seconds.

Soft Street

Every person on the challenge gets a little house, with the cat or dog they picked at sign-up sitting proudly on the roof. Make progress and the window flickers gold. Finish everything and it glows fully — everyone can see it, nobody can touch it but you. At the top you'll also see who's holding the longest streak and who's been most consistent this week.

Nudges

See a house that hasn't lit up yet? Tap "👋 nudge" underneath it to give them a gentle, public nudge in the chat. You can only nudge each person once per day, and you can't nudge yourself.

Private notes & milestones

There's a small note box on the Today tab for jotting down how the day went — it's just for you, nobody else can see it. And on days 7, 30, 50 and 75, finishing your full list unlocks a little celebration.

Recipe & Foods

A new clean-eating recipe is picked for everyone each day — no added sugar, nothing from a takeaway bag. The Foods tab is a quick reference for what's in and out.

The reset

Every window goes dark again at midnight, your time. New day, clean slate, same seven habits — and a new cheesy greeting waiting for you.

Chat

Send messages, photos, and a few celebratory stickers to cheer each other on. Be kind — someone's window might be dark for a reason.
